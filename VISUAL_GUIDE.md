## 🔧 **If You Still Don't See Videos:**

### **Check Browser Console:**
1. Open DevTools (F12)
2. Go to Console tab
3. Look for video loading errors

### **Possible Issues:**
1. **Network blocked** - Corporate firewall blocking Google Storage
2. **Browser restrictions** - Some browsers block autoplay
3. **Slow connection** - Videos take time to load (you'll see spinner)

### **Solutions:**
Videos will show a **loading spinner** first, then fade in. Even if videos fail, you'll see:
- ✅ Animated gradient backgrounds (pulsing black/gray)
- ✅ All text and content
- ✅ All buttons and navigation
- ✅ All animations
